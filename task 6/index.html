Chainsaw Man Cast

Makima

Mysterious and manipulative Public Safety officer. She’s calm, commanding, and hides terrifying secrets.

Aki Hayakawa

Stoic devil hunter with a tragic past. He’s the big brother figure to Denji and Power, driven by revenge.

Denji

- A broke teenager who merges with his chainsaw devil dog Pochita to become Chainsaw Man. He’s simple, sincere, and just wants a better life—with toast and hugs.

Reze

- Sweet café girl who charms Denji—but she’s secretly the Bomb Devil. Her duality is explosive, literally and emotionally.

Himeno

Aki’s partner with a flirty edge and deep emotional scars. Her bond with Aki is tender and heartbreaking.

Power

The chaotic Blood Fiend. Loud, proud, and surprisingly lovable. She’s Denji’s partner-in-crime and cat lover.

Angel Devil

A devil with angelic looks and a tragic touch. He drains life with contact but quietly longs for connection.

Kobeni

Kobeni Higashiyama is a shy, anxious devil hunter who constantly seems one scream away from quitting—but don’t underestimate her. Beneath the panic lies lightning-fast reflexes and surprising strength. She’s awkward, unlucky, and oddly endearing.
